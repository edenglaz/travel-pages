Discover Rome: A Travel Guide

Explore the Eternal City's rich history, architecture, and culture.

Top attractions

Colosseum

The largest amphitheater in the Roman Empire, hosting gladiator battles and public events.

Vatican City

The center of the Catholic Church, featuring St. Peter's Basilica, the Sistine Chapel, and the Vatican Museums.

Trevi Fountain

A beautiful baroque fountain where visitors can make a wish by throwing a coin into the water.

Pantheon

A magnificently preserved ancient temple, built in 126 AD, with an impressive dome and oculus.

Spanish Steps

A grand staircase connecting the Piazza di Spagna to the Trinità dei Monti church, offering stunning views of the city.
