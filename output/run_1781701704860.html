Top attractions

The Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is an iron lattice tower that offers breathtaking views of the city from its observation decks.

Coordinates: 48.8583, 2.2945

The Louvre Museum

The Louvre Museum is one of the world's largest and most famous museums, housing an impressive collection of art and artifacts.

Coordinates: 48.8611, 2.3380

Notre Dame Cathedral

Notre Dame Cathedral is a beautiful and historic Gothic church that has been a symbol of Paris for over 800 years.

Coordinates: 48.8529, 2.3501

Montmartre Neighborhood

The Montmartre neighborhood is a charming and artistic area known for its narrow streets, charming cafes, and stunning views of the city.

Coordinates: 48.8855, 2.3392

Arc de Triomphe

The Arc de Triomphe is a monumental arch that honors the soldiers who fought and died for France, offering stunning views of the city from its top.

Coordinates: 48.8738, 2.2950
