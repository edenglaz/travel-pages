Discover Tivat: A Hidden Gem in Montenegro

Tivat is a charming coastal town in Montenegro, known for its stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, and rich history. Explore the town's attractions, from ancient ruins to modern marinas.

Top attractions

Kotor Bay

A breathtaking bay surrounded by towering mountains and picturesque villages, perfect for sailing, swimming, and sunbathing.

Kotor Old Town

A medieval walled city with narrow streets, historic buildings, and stunning architecture, offering a glimpse into Tivat's rich history.

Tivat Marina

A modern marina with luxurious yachts, restaurants, and shops, offering a glimpse into the town's luxurious side.

Our Lady of the Rocks

A tiny island with a beautiful church, built on a rock in the bay, offering stunning views of Kotor Bay.

Budva Old Town

A historic walled city with narrow streets, historic buildings, and stunning architecture, offering a glimpse into Tivat's rich history and culture.
