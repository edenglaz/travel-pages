Top attractions

Colosseum

This ancient amphitheater is one of Rome's most iconic landmarks, hosting gladiator battles and public events for centuries.

Vatican City

The center of the Catholic Church, Vatican City is home to the Sistine Chapel, St. Peter's Basilica, and the Vatican Museums.

Trevi Fountain

This beautiful Baroque fountain is a must-visit, where you can toss a coin into the water to ensure your return to Rome.

Pantheon

This ancient temple is one of the best-preserved ancient buildings in Rome, featuring an impressive dome and oculus.

Spanish Steps

This grand staircase is one of Rome's most famous landmarks, offering stunning views of the city and a popular spot for people-watching.
