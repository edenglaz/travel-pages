Discover the Eternal City: A Rome Travel Guide

Explore the rich history, art, and architecture of Rome, a city that has captivated travelers for centuries.

Top attractions

The Colosseum

This ancient amphitheater is one of Rome's most iconic landmarks and a testament to the city's rich history.

The Vatican City

The Vatican is home to numerous iconic landmarks, including St. Peter's Basilica, the Sistine Chapel, and the Vatican Museums.

The Pantheon

This ancient temple is one of the best-preserved ancient buildings in Rome and features an impressive dome designed by Michelangelo.

The Trevi Fountain

This beautiful baroque fountain is a must-see attraction in Rome and offers stunning views of the surrounding cityscape.

The Roman Forum

This ancient archaeological site features a collection of ruins from ancient Rome, including temples, basilicas, and markets.
