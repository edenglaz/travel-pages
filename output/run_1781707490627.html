Discover the Charm of Madrid

Madrid, the vibrant capital of Spain, is a city that seamlessly blends tradition and modernity. From world-class museums to lively nightlife, there's something for everyone in this captivating city.

Top attractions

Museo del Prado

One of the world's premier art museums, featuring an extensive collection of European art, including works by Goya, Velázquez, and El Greco.

Royal Palace of Madrid

A grandiose palace that serves as the official residence of the Spanish royal family, offering stunning architecture and beautiful gardens.

Thyssen-Bornemisza Museum

The final component of the 'Golden Triangle of Art' in Madrid, featuring an impressive collection of modern and contemporary art.

Reina Sofia Museum

Home to Picasso's Guernica, as well as an impressive collection of modern and contemporary Spanish art.

Plaza Mayor

A historic square in the heart of Madrid, surrounded by cafes, shops, and street performers.
