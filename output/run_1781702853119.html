Discover the Eternal City: A Travel Guide to Rome

Rome, the capital of Italy, is a city steeped in history, architecture, and art. From ancient ruins to baroque fountains, Rome is a destination that will leave you in awe.

Top attractions

Colosseum

One of Rome's most iconic landmarks, the Colosseum is an ancient amphitheater that hosted gladiator battles, animal hunts, and public executions. Today, it's a must-visit attraction for history buffs and architecture enthusiasts.

Vatican City

The Vatican is home to numerous iconic landmarks, including St. Peter's Basilica, the Sistine Chapel, and the Vatican Museums. Be sure to book tickets in advance to avoid long lines.

Trevi Fountain

This beautiful baroque fountain is a must-see attraction in Rome. Legend has it that if you throw a coin into the fountain, you'll one day return to Rome.

Pantheon

This ancient temple is one of the best-preserved ancient buildings in Rome. Its impressive dome and oculus make it a must-visit attraction for architecture enthusiasts.

Spanish Steps

This grand staircase is one of Rome's most famous landmarks. Take a stroll up the steps and enjoy the beautiful views of the city.
