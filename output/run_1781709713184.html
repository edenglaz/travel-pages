Paris Travel Guide

Discover the City of Light's iconic landmarks, art museums, and romantic atmosphere.

Top attractions

The Eiffel Tower

The iron lady of Paris, a symbol of French engineering and romance.

The Louvre Museum

Home to the Mona Lisa and an extensive collection of art and artifacts.

Notre-Dame Cathedral

A beautiful and historic Gothic church that has been a Paris landmark for centuries.

Arc de Triomphe

A monumental arch honoring the soldiers who fought and died for France.

Montmartre

A charming and artistic neighborhood with narrow streets, charming cafes, and stunning views.
