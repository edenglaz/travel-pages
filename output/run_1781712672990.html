London Travel Guide

Discover the best of London, from iconic landmarks to cultural hotspots.

Top attractions

Buckingham Palace

The official residence of the British monarch, Buckingham Palace is a must-visit attraction in London. Watch the Changing of the Guard ceremony, explore the State Rooms, and learn about the palace's rich history.

The British Museum

One of the world's greatest museums, the British Museum houses a vast collection of artifacts from ancient civilizations, including the Rosetta Stone, the Elgin Marbles, and the mummies in the Ancient Egypt gallery.

The London Eye

For a panoramic view of the city, take a ride on the London Eye, a giant Ferris wheel located on the South Bank of the Thames. Enjoy breathtaking views of London's skyline and landmarks.

The National Gallery

This world-class art museum features an impressive collection of Western European art, including works by Leonardo da Vinci, Michelangelo, and Vincent van Gogh. Be sure to see Van Gogh's 'Sunflowers' and da Vinci's 'The Virgin of the Rocks'.

Westminster Abbey

This historic church has been the site of coronations, royal weddings, and state funerals for centuries. Take a tour of the abbey to see its stunning architecture, beautiful stained glass windows, and the graves of famous Britons, including Charles Dickens and Jane Austen.
