Discover the Beauty of Barcelona

Barcelona, the capital of Catalonia, is a vibrant and culturally rich city that offers a unique blend of modernism, architecture, and Mediterranean charm. From the iconic Sagrada Familia to the bustling La Rambla, Barcelona has something for every kind of traveler.

Top attractions

La Sagrada Familia

A UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most iconic landmarks in Barcelona, La Sagrada Familia is a must-visit attraction. This majestic cathedral, designed by Antoni Gaudí, is a masterpiece of Gothic and Art Nouveau styles.

Coordinates: 41.4044, 2.1749

Park Güell

Another iconic creation of Antoni Gaudí, Park Güell is a public park that features stunning architecture, beautiful gardens, and breathtaking views of the city.

Coordinates: 41.4142, 2.1525

La Rambla

The heart of Barcelona's shopping and entertainment district, La Rambla is a bustling street lined with street performers, cafes, and shops. It's a great place to experience the city's vibrant energy.

Coordinates: 41.3791, 2.1751

Casa Batlló

A house designed by Antoni Gaudí, Casa Batlló is a colorful and whimsical building that features a dragon-shaped roof and a facade made of glass and stone.

Coordinates: 41.3915, 2.1647

Barceloneta Beach

A popular beach destination in Barcelona, Barceloneta Beach offers a relaxed atmosphere, clear waters, and stunning views of the Mediterranean Sea.

Coordinates: 41.3793, 2.1930
