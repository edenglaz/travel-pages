Discover Tivat, Montenegro

A charming coastal town in Montenegro, Tivat is a popular destination for its stunning beaches, historic sites, and vibrant culture.

Top attractions

Porto Montenegro

A luxurious marina and yacht club that offers a range of amenities and services, including shopping, dining, and entertainment options.

Tivat Bay

A picturesque bay with a beautiful beach and crystal-clear waters, perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and water sports.

Sveti Ilija Church

A historic Orthodox church that dates back to the 19th century and features stunning architecture and beautiful views of the surrounding area.

Tivat Old Town

A charming historic district with narrow streets, old buildings, and a lively atmosphere, perfect for exploring and discovering local culture.

Kotor Bay

A stunning bay with breathtaking scenery, crystal-clear waters, and a range of outdoor activities, including hiking, biking, and kayaking.
