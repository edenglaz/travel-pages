Discover Rome: The Eternal City

Rome, the capital of Italy, is a city steeped in history and architecture. From ancient ruins to baroque fountains, Rome is a must-visit destination for any traveler.

Gallery

Top attractions

The Colosseum

This ancient amphitheater is one of Rome's most iconic landmarks and a testament to the city's rich history.

The Vatican City

Home to numerous iconic landmarks, including St. Peter's Basilica and the Sistine Chapel, the Vatican is a must-visit destination for art and history lovers.

The Pantheon

This ancient temple is one of the best-preserved ancient buildings in Rome and features an impressive dome designed by Michelangelo.

The Trevi Fountain

This beautiful baroque fountain is a popular spot for tourists and locals alike, and legend has it that if you throw a coin into the fountain, you'll one day return to Rome.

The Spanish Steps

This grand staircase is one of the city's most famous landmarks and offers stunning views of the surrounding cityscape.
