Discover Tivat: A Hidden Gem on the Bay of Kotor

Tivat is a charming coastal town in Montenegro, known for its stunning bay, beautiful beaches, and rich history. Explore the town's attractions, from ancient ruins to modern marinas, and experience the warm hospitality of the locals.

Top attractions

Porto Montenegro

A luxury marina and yacht club that offers a range of amenities, including restaurants, bars, and shopping. Take a stroll along the marina and admire the stunning yachts and boats.

Tivat Old Town

A historic town center with narrow streets, charming shops, and traditional restaurants. Explore the old town and discover its rich history and culture.

Budva Old Town

A UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the best-preserved old towns in the Balkans. Visit the old town and explore its narrow streets, historic buildings, and beautiful beaches.

Kotor Bay

A stunning bay with crystal-clear waters, surrounded by mountains and picturesque villages. Take a boat tour or rent a kayak to explore the bay and its many coves and beaches.

Ostrog Monastery

A historic monastery built into the side of a mountain, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding countryside. Visit the monastery and learn about its rich history and significance.
