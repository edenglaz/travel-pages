Discover Tivat: A Hidden Gem on the Bay of Kotor

Tivat is a charming coastal town in Montenegro, known for its stunning natural beauty, rich history, and vibrant culture.

Top attractions

Porto Montenegro

A luxury marina and yacht club that offers a range of amenities and activities, including shopping, dining, and boat tours.

Tivat Bay

A picturesque bay with crystal-clear waters, surrounded by lush greenery and scenic hiking trails.

Kotor Bay

A stunning bay with towering mountains and a rich history, offering a range of outdoor activities and cultural experiences.

Tivat Old Town

A charming historic center with narrow streets, colorful buildings, and a range of shops, restaurants, and cafes.

Villa Bocche

A beautiful 19th-century villa that now serves as a museum, offering a glimpse into the town's rich history and cultural heritage.
