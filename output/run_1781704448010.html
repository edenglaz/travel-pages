Discover the Wonders of Moscow

Experience the rich history, vibrant culture, and breathtaking architecture of Moscow, the capital city of Russia.

Top attractions

The Kremlin

A UNESCO World Heritage Site and the official residence of the President of Russia, the Kremlin is a must-visit attraction in Moscow. Explore the stunning palaces, cathedrals, and museums within its walls.

Coordinates: 55.7516, 37.6181

Red Square

A historic heart of Moscow, Red Square is home to iconic landmarks such as St. Basil's Cathedral, Lenin's Mausoleum, and the State Historical Museum. Take a stroll through the square and soak up the vibrant atmosphere.

Coordinates: 55.7536, 37.6215

Tretyakov Gallery

One of the world's greatest museums of Russian art, the Tretyakov Gallery features an impressive collection of paintings, sculptures, and other artworks from the 11th to the 20th centuries.

Coordinates: 55.7414, 37.6202

Bolshoi Theatre

Experience the magic of Russian ballet and opera at the world-famous Bolshoi Theatre, known for its stunning architecture and rich history.

Coordinates: 55.7601, 37.6186

Arbat Street

A lively pedestrian street lined with shops, cafes, and street performers, Arbat Street is a great place to explore Moscow's street culture and nightlife.

Coordinates: 55.7535, 37.6123
