Discover Moscow: A City of Grandeur and Culture

Explore the rich history, architecture, and vibrant culture of Moscow, the capital city of Russia.

Top attractions

The Kremlin

The Kremlin is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and the official residence of the President of Russia, featuring historic palaces, cathedrals, and museums.

Red Square

Red Square is a historic city square in the heart of Moscow, surrounded by iconic landmarks such as St. Basil's Cathedral and the Kremlin.

Tretyakov Gallery

The Tretyakov Gallery is one of Russia's largest and most famous art museums, showcasing a vast collection of Russian art from the 11th to the 20th centuries.

Bolshoi Theatre

The Bolshoi Theatre is a world-renowned opera and ballet house, offering stunning performances of classical and modern productions.

Moscow Metro

The Moscow Metro is one of the most beautiful and extensive metro systems in the world, featuring stunning architecture and ornate decorations.
