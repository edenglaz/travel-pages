Discover the Magic of London

London, the capital of England, is a city steeped in history, culture, and entertainment. From iconic landmarks to world-class museums, there's something for everyone in this vibrant city.

Top attractions

Buckingham Palace

The official residence of the British monarch, Buckingham Palace is a must-visit attraction in London. Watch the Changing of the Guard ceremony, explore the State Rooms, and enjoy the beautiful gardens.

The British Museum

One of the world's greatest museums, the British Museum houses a vast collection of artifacts from ancient civilizations, including the Rosetta Stone, the Elgin Marbles, and the mummies in the Ancient Egypt gallery.

The London Eye

For a panoramic view of the city, take a ride on the London Eye, a giant Ferris wheel located on the South Bank of the Thames. Enjoy the stunning views of London's skyline and landmarks.

The National Gallery

This world-class art museum houses a collection of over 2,300 paintings, including works by Leonardo da Vinci, Michelangelo, and Vincent van Gogh. Don't miss the famous 'Sunflowers' by Van Gogh.

The Tower of London

This historic fortress has served as a palace, prison, armory, and even a zoo over the centuries. Today, it's home to the Crown Jewels and the Yeoman Warders (also known as Beefeaters).
