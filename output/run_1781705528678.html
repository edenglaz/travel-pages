Discover the Wonders of London

Explore the iconic landmarks, rich history, and vibrant culture of London, a city that has something for everyone.

Top attractions

Buckingham Palace

The official residence of the British monarch, Buckingham Palace is a must-visit attraction in London. Watch the Changing of the Guard ceremony, explore the State Rooms, and learn about the history of the Royal Family.

Coordinates: 51.5008, -0.1419

The British Museum

One of the world's greatest museums, the British Museum houses a vast collection of artifacts from ancient civilizations, including the Rosetta Stone, the Elgin Marbles, and the mummies in the Ancient Egypt gallery.

Coordinates: 51.5194, -0.1269

The London Eye

For a panoramic view of the city, take a ride on the London Eye, a giant Ferris wheel located on the South Bank of the Thames. Enjoy breathtaking views of London's skyline and landmarks.

Coordinates: 51.5033, -0.1194

The National Gallery

This world-class art museum features an impressive collection of Western European art, including works by Leonardo da Vinci, Michelangelo, and Vincent van Gogh. Be sure to see Van Gogh's 'Sunflowers' and da Vinci's 'The Virgin of the Rocks'.

Coordinates: 51.5089, -0.1283

The Tower of London

This historic fortress has served as a palace, prison, armory, and even a zoo over the centuries. Today, it's home to the Crown Jewels and the Yeoman Warders (also known as Beefeaters). Don't miss the Ceremony of the Keys, a 700-year-old tradition.

Coordinates: 51.5081, -0.0761
