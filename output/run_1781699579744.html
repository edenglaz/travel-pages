Discover Lisbon: A Charming Portuguese City

Lisbon, the capital of Portugal, is a city steeped in history, culture, and natural beauty. From its picturesque hills to its vibrant neighborhoods, Lisbon has something to offer every kind of traveler.

Gallery

Top attractions

Belem Tower

This medieval fortress and UNESCO World Heritage Site is a must-visit attraction in Lisbon, offering stunning views of the Tagus River and the city.

Castelo de Sao Jorge

Perched atop a hill in the historic Alfama neighborhood, this Moorish castle offers breathtaking views of the city and is a great place to learn about Lisbon's rich history.

Coordinates: 38.7139, -9.1335

National Pantheon

This beautiful Baroque church is the final resting place of many notable Portuguese figures, including writers, artists, and politicians.

Lisbon Oceanarium

One of the largest aquariums in Europe, the Lisbon Oceanarium features an incredible array of marine life from around the world, including sharks, rays, and sea turtles.

Miradouro de Santa Luzia

This scenic viewpoint in the historic Alfama neighborhood offers stunning views of the city and the Tagus River, making it a great place to watch the sunset.

Coordinates: 38.7117, -9.1302
