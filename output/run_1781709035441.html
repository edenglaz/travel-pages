Discover the Eternal City: A Rome Travel Guide

Explore the rich history, architecture, and culture of Rome, from ancient ruins to Baroque masterpieces.

Top attractions

Colosseum

The largest amphitheater in the Roman Empire, hosting gladiator battles and public events.

Vatican City

An independent city-state within Rome, home to numerous iconic landmarks, including St. Peter's Basilica and the Sistine Chapel.

Trevi Fountain

A beautiful baroque fountain, famous for its stunning architecture and the tradition of throwing coins.

Pantheon

A magnificently preserved ancient temple, built in 126 AD, featuring an impressive dome and oculus.

Spanish Steps

A grand staircase connecting the Piazza di Spagna to the Trinità dei Monti church, offering breathtaking views of the city.
