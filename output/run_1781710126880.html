Discover Tivat: A Hidden Gem on the Montenegrin Coast

Tivat is a charming coastal town in Montenegro, known for its stunning beaches, picturesque harbor, and rich history. Explore the town's many attractions and experience the warm hospitality of the locals.

Top attractions

Kotor Bay

A breathtaking bay surrounded by towering mountains and picturesque villages, perfect for sailing, swimming, and exploring.

Tivat Marina

A modern and bustling marina that offers a range of amenities, including restaurants, shops, and boat tours.

Our Lady of the Rocks

A beautiful island church located in the middle of the bay, famous for its stunning architecture and picturesque surroundings.

Kotor Old Town

A medieval walled city that is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, featuring narrow streets, historic buildings, and stunning views of the bay.

Tivat Beach

A long and sandy beach that is perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and water sports, with a range of amenities and restaurants nearby.
