Discover the Eternal City: A Rome Travel Guide

Explore the rich history, architecture, and culture of Rome, from ancient ruins to Vatican City.

Top attractions

Colosseum

This ancient amphitheater is one of Rome's most iconic landmarks and a testament to the city's rich history.

Vatican City

The center of the Catholic Church, Vatican City is home to numerous iconic landmarks, including St. Peter's Basilica and the Sistine Chapel.

Pantheon

This ancient temple is one of the best-preserved ancient buildings in Rome and a masterpiece of Roman architecture.

Trevi Fountain

This beautiful baroque fountain is a must-see in Rome, and legend has it that if you throw a coin into the fountain, you'll one day return to the city.

Spanish Steps

One of the city's most famous landmarks, the Spanish Steps are a beautiful example of 18th-century architecture and a popular spot for people-watching.
