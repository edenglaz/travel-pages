Top attractions

Eiffel Tower

The iron lattice tower is a symbol of Paris and offers breathtaking views of the city.

Coordinates: 48.8583, 2.2945

Louvre Museum

Home to the Mona Lisa and an extensive collection of European art and artifacts.

Notre-Dame Cathedral

A beautiful Gothic church that has been a Parisian landmark for over 850 years.

Arc de Triomphe

A monumental arch honoring the soldiers who fought and died for France, offering stunning views of the city.

Coordinates: 48.8738, 2.2950

Montmartre

A historic and artistic neighborhood with narrow streets, charming cafes, and stunning views of the city.

Coordinates: 48.8855, 2.3392
